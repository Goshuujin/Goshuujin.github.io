David Allen is a military veteran and recent college graduate. He has earned a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science from the University of California in Berkeley. He has experience with such programming languages as C/C++, Java, Javascript, and Python. Working with a team of students David Allen helped create the auto grading functionality for the visual based programming language Snap!. Snap! is used in the online course The Beauty and Joy of Computing found on EdX.org. Currently David Allen resides in Berkeley, California.
